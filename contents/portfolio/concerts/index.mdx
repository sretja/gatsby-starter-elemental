First Round Management was founded in 2008 by current President and C.E.O. Malki Kawa, recognized as the top agent in the sport of Mixed Martial Arts (MMA).

The Miami, Fla.-based firm has grown to today represent the top fighters in all of MMA, including in the UFC, the world's top promotion. With a team of highly-skilled professionals, First Round Management assists fighters in every step of their career, from booking the first fight to the public relations following the championship victory.
